    Why Do I Do What I Do?

    The Short Answer

    I like learning and helping others.

    The Long Answer

    I love to learn new things and apply them to the things I’m interested in (video games, aesthetics, storytelling, etc).

    Learning how people work and how their stories unfold is fascinating. And using insights garnered from studying them to improve their lives (whether it be functionally or just sparking some joy) excites me and pushes me to fight for the user.

    That’s why I design—to help people—even if it’s just a little bit.
